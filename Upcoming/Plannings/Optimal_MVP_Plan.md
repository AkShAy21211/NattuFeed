# 🌴 NattuFeed: The Hyper-Local Operating System for Kerala

NattuFeed is an Extreme Premium Hyperlocal Platform designed to solve high-friction daily problems in Kerala wards through "Community Intelligence." It transforms the passive information found in unorganized WhatsApp groups into a real-time, verified dashboard for transit, economy, and local accountability.

## 🎯 The MVP Vision: "Utility over Bureaucracy"
To win against the "Mute Culture" of WhatsApp, NattuFeed focuses on three High-Frequency, Low-Trust features that provide immediate value to residents without needing official government integration on Day 1.

### 1. Improved Bus Spott (The Virtual Radar)
Since private buses—which serve 70% of Kerala's passengers—lack official GPS tracking, NattuFeed uses a Human-Powered Radar system.

- **Reporter Flow**: A user on the bus (e.g., Azhikode-Kannur Limited Stop) taps one of two high-contrast buttons: **[ To City ]** or **[ To Village ]**.
- **The GPS Snap**: The app silently grabs the user's coordinates and creates a "Spott" card.
- **Commuter Flow**: A user at the stop sees a card showing: "Kannur Bus: 800m away (Approaching)."
- **The Countdown Bar**: Every Spott has an animated green progress bar that shrinks over 20 minutes. If the bar is gone, the info is "dead."
- **Verification Chain**: If a second user taps "Me Too" further down the route, the bus's location updates on the radar, making it "move" for everyone in the ward.

### 2. Market Price Intelligence (Economic Power)
Kerala’s retail markets suffer from high price instability and "monopsony" conditions where a few traders control prices.

- **The Mechanism**: When posting in the Market category, users are given a dedicated numeric field for price.
- **Local Averages**: The app calculates the Mean Average of all reports for a specific item (e.g., Sardine, Rubber, or Coconut) from the last 12 hours.
- **Formula**: `Avg = (Σ Price) / n`
- **Midnight Reset**: To ensure "Freshness," the ticker resets daily at midnight.

### 3. Grievance Audit (Public Accountability)
Official portals like K-Smart often suffer from "mechanical closures" where complaints are closed without being fixed.

- **The Status Toggle**: Every Public Service post (e.g., broken street light) starts as **[Pending]**.
- **The Victory Moment**: Once fixed, the Original Author or the Founder (Admin) can toggle it to **[Resolved]**.
- **Social Proof**: High "Me Too" counts on a grievance create a "Public To-Do List" that is harder for local officials to ignore than a private complaint.

## 🛡️ The "Human Firewall" (Zero-Budget Security)
To protect the app from bad actors and "lazy clicking" without paying for expensive security APIs, we use internal logic:

| Feature | Invisible Guardrail | Technical Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spam Control** | The 3-Flag Rule | If a post receives 3 reports, it is auto-hidden from the feed for everyone except the Founder. |
| **Lazy Clicking** | Proximity Gating | The "Me Too" button is disabled if the user's GPS is more than 500m away from the reported event. |
| **Fake Reports** | Delayed Karma | Karma points stay "Pending." If a post is later flagged as "Fake/Gone," the verifier loses 50 Karma. |
| **Spam Speed** | Velocity Limit | A 5-minute cooldown is enforced between transit verifications to stop "Karma Farming." |

## 🎨 Cultural Design (Nadumuttam Vibe)
NattuFeed avoids the "Gov App" feel by using a premium, culturally rooted design system:

- **Traditional Motif**: An open, airy layout inspired by the *Nadumuttam* (central courtyard).
- **Earthy Palette**: Modern glassmorphism layered over colors like Clay Red, Ivory, and Deep Brown.
- **Casual Language**: Using Malayalam-casual terms (e.g., "Bus Evide?" instead of "Transit Query").

## 💰 Sustainability Roadmap (Post-Launch)
Once the app achieves "Product-Market Fit" (approx. 100+ active ward users):
- **Featured Deals**: Local vendors pay a small fee (e.g., ₹20) to "Pin" their price report.
- **Verified Pro Directory**: A "Need a Plumber?" button on utility grievances that links to community-vetted workers.
- **Kudumbashree Marketplace**: A "Today's Specials" section for local women-led micro-enterprises.

# Journey 4: Gamification & Safety

The loop that keeps the community healthy and vibrant.

### 1. Earning Status (The Karma Loop)
- **Action**: User consistently posts "Bus Spotts" or "Market Prices" that are trust-verified by neighbors.
- **Trust Anchor (Proximity Gating)**: Verification points for Bus Spotts are only awarded if the verifier is within **500m** of the bus location, preventing remote gaming of the system.
- **Feedback**: Profile photo gets a "Silver" then "Gold" outline.
- **The Podium**: User appears on the **Top 10 Leaderboard Card** with rank-specific glows and animations.
- **Benefit**: Higher Karma users gain community respect and unlocked seniority badges.

### 2. The Reporting Loop (Safety)
- **Action**: A user sees an abusive post -> Taps `Report`.
- **Threshold**: After 3 reports, the post vanishes from the feed.
- **Moderation**: You (The Admin) receive a notification to "Delete" or "Shield" the post.

### 3. The Installation (PWA Journey)
- **Safari/Chrome**: App detects it's not "Installed."
- **UI Prompt**: A subtle bottom drawer with an arrow pointing to the "Add to Home Screen" button.
- **End Result**: NattuFeed sits on the neighbor's home screen with its premium teal icon.

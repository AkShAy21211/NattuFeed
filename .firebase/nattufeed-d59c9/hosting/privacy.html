Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

NattuFeed is a hyperlocal community app for neighborhoods in Kerala. We are committed to being transparent about what data we collect and why. This policy explains our practices in plain language.

What We Collect

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo obtained from your Google account when you sign in.

  • Location data: Your precise GPS coordinates, collected only when the app is open and in use. This is required to show you posts from your local area (~2km radius). We do not track your location in the background.

  • Content you create: Posts, headlines, and details you submit to the feed.

  • Usage data: Which posts you verify ("Me Too") or flag. This is used to calculate your Karma score.

How We Use Your Data

  • To show you a real-time feed of posts within your neighborhood.

  • To display your name and photo on posts you create and on the leaderboard.

  • To calculate and display your Karma score.

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not serve advertisements based on your data.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google LLC): We use Firebase Authentication to manage your login, and Cloud Firestore to store your account data and posts. Your data is stored on Google's servers in the asia-south1 (Mumbai, India) region.

  • Google Sign-In: When you log in with Google, we receive only your name, email, and profile photo.

  • These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and erase your personal data.

  • To delete your account and all associated data, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent.

  • To request a copy of your data or raise a concern, contact us at nimbact@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at nimbact@gmail.com
